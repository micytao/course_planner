# UC 2026 Calendar — Detailed Degree Rules

## BA (Bachelor of Arts) — 360 pts

- Schedule V: 225 pts minimum
- Schedule C: ARTS102, MAOR165, WRIT101 (30 pts total, cannot count toward major/minor)
- Electives: 105 pts from any degree
- 225 pts above 100-level, 90 pts at 300-level
- Pathway: (a) 1 major + 1 minor, (b) 2 majors, (c) 1 specialisation
- Major: 135 pts (60 at 300-level, 45+ at 200-level)
- Minor: 75 pts (45 at 200+)
- No double-counting courses between majors
- 30+ majors, 7 specialisations

**BA Specialisations** (alternative to major+minor, 225 pts each):
Creative Industries, Cultural Heritage, Global Societies, International Affairs, Language Brain & Behaviour, Philosophy Politics & Economics, Society Diversity & Change

## BCom (Bachelor of Commerce) — 360 pts

- Schedule V: 255 pts, Schedule C: 105 pts
- Schedule C (all Year 1): ACCT102, ECON104, INFO123, MGMT100, STAT101, BSNS201 (requires 60 pts), BSNS299 (0 pts, requires 180 pts)
- 225 above 100-level, 90 at 300-level
- 1 major required, optional minor
- 11 majors: Accounting, Economics, Finance, HRM, Information Systems, Innovation & Entrepreneurship, International Business, Management, Marketing, Operations & Supply Chain Management, Taxation & Accounting
- Minor-only: Business Analytics, Business and Sustainability, Entrepreneurship, Innovation

## BE(Hons) (Bachelor of Engineering with Honours) — 480 pts

- Year 1 common core (120 pts): COSC131, EMTH118, EMTH119, ENGR100 (0 pts), ENGR101, PHYS101
- Discipline extras by discipline:
  - Chemical/Civil/Forest/Mechanical: CHEM111
  - Civil/Forest/Mechanical/Mechatronics: ENGR102
  - Computer/Electrical/Software: COSC122 or MATH120
- Bridge courses: EMTH117 (maths), PHYS111 (physics), CHEM114 (chemistry)
- Intro courses max 2 attempts; must gain Year 2 within 2 years
- Years 2-4 fully prescribed per discipline
- ENGR200 work experience (0 pts)
- Engineering minors: Bioprocess/Sustainable Energy/Environmental Process (Chemical), Structural/Water & Environmental (Civil), Communications & Network (Computer), Power (Electrical), Aerospace/Biomedical (Mechanical)

## BHlth (Bachelor of Health) — 360 pts (NEW 2026)

- Schedule C: HLTH101, HLTH105, HLTH106, HLTH203, HLTH302, HLTH321 (internship)
- 225 above 100-level, 90 at 300-level
- 8 majors: Exercise & Sport Nutrition, Health Education, Health Science, Māori & Indigenous Health, Mental Health & Addiction, Physical Activity & Wellbeing, Psychology, Public Health
- Mental Health & Addiction: practicum requires police vetting + Children's Act

## BSW(Hons) (Bachelor of Social Work with Honours) — 480 pts

- Fixed programme: 390 pts Schedule C + 90 pts Schedule E
- No majors/minors
- Progression gates: 240 pts before 300-level; SOWK301 + SOWK308 before 400-level
- Special admission with police vetting at 200/300/400-level
- IELTS 6.5 (no band below 6.5) for non-native English speakers
- Field Education: SOWK491/492 in final year (45 pts each)

## BSport (Bachelor of Sport) — 360 pts

- Schedule C: 135 pts + major from Schedule S Group 1
- 6 majors: Physical Activity & Wellbeing, Marketing & Management, Performance Data Analysis, Sport Coaching & PE, Strength & Conditioning, Te Ao Hākinakina
- Practicum chain: SPRT110 → SPRT210 → SPRT310/SPRT320
- Police vetting, First Aid cert, Children's Act required

## BTchLn (Ako: Bachelor of Teaching and Learning) — 360 pts

- Endorsement model (not majors): Early Childhood, Primary, Mātauranga Māori
- All Schedule C + 300 pts from endorsement group
- Special application by 1 Dec (domestic) / 31 Oct (international)
- Interview, literacy/numeracy tests, referee reports, police vetting, Children's Act
- Fixed year-by-year programme per endorsement
- Professional practice courses: max 2 attempts

## LLB (Bachelor of Laws) — 480 pts

- No majors/minors, prescribed programme
- Year 1: LAWS101 (30 pts) + LAWS110 (15 pts) + 75 pts non-law
- Year 2: LAWS203/204/205/206 (120 pts) — limited entry (310–350 places), apply by 1 Dec
- Year 3-4: LAWS202, LAWS301 + electives from Schedule E
- Almost always double degree: LLB/BA, LLB/BCom, LLB/BSc
- Cross-credit: 120 pts with BA (incl. LAWS101), 30 pts with BCom
- Bar admission: LAWS398 Legal Ethics + NZCLE programme

## BCJ (Bachelor of Criminal Justice) — 360 pts

- Fixed programme: 240 pts Schedule C + 15 pts Schedule E Group 1 + 105 pts Schedule E Group 2
- No majors/minors
- Core: CRJU101, CRJU150, CRJU160, CRJU201, CRJU202, CRJU301, CRJU302, HSRV103, HSRV104, MAOR219, PHIL139, PSYC105, PSYC106, SOCI293

## BSc (Bachelor of Science) — 360 pts

- Schedule V: 255 pts, Schedule C: SCIE101 + 15 pts Group 2
- 225 above 100-level, 90 at 300-level
- 1 major + optional minor (or 2 majors)
- 19 majors including Computer Science, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Psychology, Statistics
- Environmental Science requires a second major

## BDataSc (Bachelor of Data Science) — 360 pts

- Schedule C: 165 pts Group 1 (COSC/DATA/MATH/SCIE) + 15 pts Group 2 (STAT201/202) + 15 pts Group 3 (STAT315/318)
- 225 above 100-level, 105 at 300-level
- 5 majors: Bioinformatics, Business Analytics, Data Science, Population Health Data Science, Spatial Data Science

## BPsycSc (Bachelor of Psychological Science) — 360 pts

- Schedule C: 195 pts (PSYC105/106/107, SPSC114, PSYC206/207/208/213/215, PSYC375/377/379) + 15 pts from HLTH106/MAOR108/TREO110/TREO111
- Unique: minors instead of majors
- 6 Group 1 minors: Forensic Psychology, Māori & Indigenous Perspectives, Neuroscience & Cognition, Psychological Wellbeing, Psychology for Common Good, Workplace Psychology
- Must complete 1 Group 1 minor + 2nd minor or 60 pts electives

## Other Arts Degrees

- **BC** (360 pts): 4 majors. Core: COMS101, COMS105, COMS201, COMS207, COMS231, COMS330, DIGI204, WRIT101 + MAOR108/MAOR165/TREO110
- **BDigiScreen(Hons)** (480 pts): 6 majors (Animation, Cinematic Arts, Game Arts, Game Development, Screen Sound, Screenwriting). B average at 300-level for 400-level progression.
- **BSEnS** (360 pts): Schedule C: ENVR101, MAOR108, SENS101, SENS201, SENS301 + COMS105/WRIT101. 4 majors.
- **BFA** (480 pts): Limited entry (78 places, portfolio by 15 Nov). 5 studio majors (Graphic Design, Moving Image, Painting, Photography, Sculpture).
- **MusB** (360 pts): 4 majors (Composition, Music Production, Music Studies, Performance). Audition for Performance.

## BYCL (Bachelor of Youth and Community Leadership) — 360 pts

- Schedule C: 150 pts + Schedule E: 105 pts
- No major, optional external minor
- Standard admission
